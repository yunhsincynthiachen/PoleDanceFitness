About Us

We are a group of Olin College Engineering Students, who were interested in taking Pole-Dancing Fitness Classes for a passionate pursuit. There's a lot of taboo about Pole-Dancing, even though it is a form of eercise that takes strength, flexibility and endurance. It has also been shown to inspire body confidence, so we were interested in understanding the experience, difficulty and empowerment that comes along with this form of dance.
